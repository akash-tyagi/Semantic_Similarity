Bill Gates vacations better than most aboard 450-foot luxury yacht near Italy

Moored off the coast of Sardinia, the boat Gates and family are renting reportedly has 12 state rooms, a seawater swimming pool, a cinema, a nightclub, a climbing wall, a gym and two hot tubs.

BY Lee Moran

Friday, August 8, 2014, 11:55 AM

Bill Gates doesn’t slum it when it comes to his vacations.

The world's richest man has been photographed enjoying quality family time aboard an extravagant $330 million yacht.

The Microsoft founder has been pictured on “The Serene,” a stunning 450-foot boat he’s renting from Stolichnaya vodka magnate Yuri Scheffler for a cool $5 million per week.

Moored off the coast of Sardinia, it reportedly has 12 state rooms, a seawater swimming pool, a cinema, a nightclub, a climbing wall, a gym and two hot tubs.

There's also a helicopter to fly the clan to and from tennis matches in the nearby “billionaires' playground” of Porto Cervo — and a submarine for a spot of underwater exploration.

And to relax, there's a full library, a health spa and a tranquil underwater viewing room.

Gates, who's worth an eye-popping $76 billion, is reportedly with wife Melinda and children Rory, Jennifer and Phoebe on the trip.
